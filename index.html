About Dr. Kacha

Expert care rooted in empathy

Dr. Tanvi Kacha is a well-known Mental and Behavioural Science specialist currently associated with GMERS Medical College and Hospital, Mullawada, Junagadh. With over 9 years of clinical experience, she has worked across multiple reputed hospitals and cities across India.

She is recognized for her attention to accurate diagnosis and for treating patients with deep empathy. Dr. Kacha has a special interest in autism and child psychiatry, and has actively participated in research, workshops, and published several papers in the field of mental and behavioural sciences.

She has also been awarded in the field of Mental & Behavioural Science and holds a fellowship in Implementation Research in Mental Health (2020).

What I Offer

Comprehensive psychiatric care

Evidence-based treatment for a full spectrum of mental health conditions.

😔
Depression & Mood Disorders
Personalized evaluation and treatment for major depression, bipolar disorder, and dysthymia using the latest evidence-based approaches.
🌀
Anxiety & Panic Disorders
CBT-based and pharmacological strategies for generalized anxiety, OCD, panic attacks, social anxiety, and phobias.
👶
Child & Adolescent Psychiatry
Special interest in autism spectrum disorder (ASD), ADHD, childhood anxiety, and mental health issues in vulnerable children.
🧩
Autism Spectrum Disorder
Comprehensive assessment, diagnosis, and management of autism in children and adolescents with a family-centred approach.

Medication Management
Expert psychiatric medication evaluation and ongoing monitoring to ensure safe, effective, and optimized therapeutic outcomes.
🧪
Psychotherapy & Counselling
Individual and supportive therapy sessions to address trauma, stress, grief, relationship issues, and life transitions.

Behavioural Science Disorders

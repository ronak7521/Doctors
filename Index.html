About Dr. Voss

Healing rooted in science and humanity

Dr. Serena Voss is a board-certified psychiatrist with over 15 years of clinical experience. A graduate of Johns Hopkins School of Medicine, she completed her residency at Massachusetts General Hospital and fellowship at UCSF.

She believes mental health care should be accessible, compassionate, and evidence-based. Her integrative approach combines the latest in psychiatric medicine with mindfulness, therapy, and lifestyle medicine for lasting wellness.
